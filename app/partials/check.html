Notes

If you have more than one Form 1099-K for your business, add up the gross receipts and transation counts before you enter the information:

  • Enter your business's MCC and ZIP code. If your business has multiple MCC's, use the category code with the highest gross receipts.
  • Enter total payment card receits from Form 1099-K(s)
  • Enter total transaction count from 1099K(s)
  • Enter gross receipts for the business - remember to include all sources of income, including non-card sources such as cash and checks.
